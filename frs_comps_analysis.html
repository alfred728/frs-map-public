Strengths vs Peers

  • Resource 826koz resource base — comparable to Catalyst (CYL), growing via acquisitions

Valuation Gap Opportunity

  • Re-rate Trigger 1 First gold pour at Lake Johnston (Nov 2026) — transition from explorer to producer
  • Re-rate Trigger 2 Steady-state production at ~100koz p.a. — enters peer group comps range
  • Re-rate Trigger 3 Edna May acquisition — doubles production to ~200koz, re-rates into mid-tier space
  • Re-rate Trigger 4 Resource growth via Lake Rebecca (16 tenements) and ongoing drilling
