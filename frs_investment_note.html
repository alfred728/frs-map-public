Forrestania Resources is an opportunity to co-invest alongside one of the most dynamic management teams we have seen on ASX. Everyone Wants to Invest Alongside Geraghty
 

The strongest signal is not what management says. It is what the people around them do with their own capital. VM Drilling was awarded a 90,000 metre RC drilling program for 2026 and elected to take the first six months of fees in FRS shares rather than cash. Polaris Engineering is delivering the A$58.5 million Lake Johnston CIL plant conversion and chose to be paid entirely in FRS scrip, issued at a 12% premium to the 10-day VWAP. Kerry Stokes, through Wroxby Pty Ltd, took a cornerstone position in the November 2025 capital raise and now holds 9.5% of the company. Tim Roberts is also on the register. Capital raising risk for the near term has been substantially reduced.

Geraghty and the board are heavily invested in the stock. He, alongside Technical Director Brett Hodgins, and Non-Executive Directors Daniel Raihani and Adam Turnbull, collectively hold approximately 136 million ordinary shares. He has also shown discipline: after taking a strategic stake in TG Metals, he walked away from the full acquisition when the deal terms did not stack up. Aggression and discipline in equal measure.
